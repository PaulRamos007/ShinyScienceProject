Moving along... beyond the solar system, we're in inter-stellar space. But we're still at home in our MILKY WAY galaxy!

  • Our universer contains BILLIONS of galaxies, which come in different shapes.
  • Spiral – spiral arms, younger stars, and new stars still being created in spiral galaxies. Our milky way is a spiral galaxy.
  • Elliptical - The stars found in Elliptical Galaxies are often very old. This is because elliptical galaxies don't actively create new stars. The only stars found with in them were created along time ago.
  • Irregular – evolved differently or became irregular after colliding with another galaxy
  • QUESTION. What is at the center of our Galaxy?
  • ANSWER. Super massive black hole – Size: 4 million times the size of our sun - Distance: 26000 light years away from us so no danger to us
