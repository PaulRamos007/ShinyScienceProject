Which star is closest to earth anyway? Is it Alpha-Centauri, Alpha-Proximi?

  • Actually, the closest star to us the OUR STAR - THE SUN.
  • It's the center of our solar system, and it's impressive gravity keeps eight planets and a bunch of other rocks, gas and dust inside our solar system.
  • Is there wind in space? Well, there's no air to move around, so you're probably thinking 'no'.
  • However, there is a force called the SOLAR WIND, the flow of particles and plasma from the sun!!
  • Lucky for us, earth's magnetic field diverts the solar wind around us, or we'd be in SERIOUS TROUBLE.
